About fpsStats

fpsStats is a powerful tool designed to help gamers improve their performance and track their statistics in popular first-person shooter games like Fortnite and Apex Legends. Whether you're a casual player or a competitive gamer, fpsStats provides valuable insights and analysis that can elevate your gameplay to the next level. With fpsStats, you can easily monitor your in-game performance by tracking important metrics such as kills, deaths, wins, accuracy, and more. By analyzing these statistics, you can identify your strengths and weaknesses, pinpoint areas that need improvement, and develop strategies to enhance your gameplay.

Currently Supported Games

Fortnite

Fortnite is a popular online multiplayer video game developed by Epic Games. It belongs to the genre of battle royale games, where a large number of players compete against each other in a free-for-all style combat until only one player or team remains victorious. The game takes place in a virtual world where players are dropped onto a large island and are required to scavenge for weapons, resources, and other items to help them survive and eliminate their opponents. The playable area gradually shrinks over time, forcing players into closer proximity and intensifying the battles.

Apex Legends

Apex Legends is a popular free-to-play online multiplayer battle royale game developed by Respawn Entertainment and published by Electronic Arts. It takes place in a futuristic science-fiction universe known as the Outlands, where legendary individuals called "Legends" compete in a high-stakes battle for fame, fortune, and glory. In Apex Legends, players form squads of three and enter a fast-paced, team-based battle royale experience. Each player selects a unique Legend, each with their own distinct abilities, playstyles, and backstory. The Legends possess diverse tactical and ultimate abilities that can turn the tide of battle and offer strategic advantages to their teams.
